Radiohead Website

My favourite three album releases are:

The Bends
The Bends was released in 1995. It was produced by John Leckie, and engineered
by Nigel Godrich, who has produced all of Radiohead's subsequent studio albums.
Amnesiac
Amnesiac was released in 2001. Recorded with producer Nigel Godrich during the
same sessions as Radiohead's previous album Kid A, Amnesiac incorporates similar
influences of electronic music, 20th-century classical music, jazz and krautrock.
OK Computer
OK Computer was released in 1997. The members of Radiohead self-produced the
album with Nigel Godrich.
